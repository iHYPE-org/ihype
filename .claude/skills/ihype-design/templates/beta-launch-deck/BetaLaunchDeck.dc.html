Stripe processes payments directly — the card fee (2.9% + $0.30; AMEX 3.5% + $0.30) is the only charge above face value. The promoter's up-to-10% is its own dedicated slice — iHYPE takes 0%, never touching the artist's 70%. Default venue split is configurable per charter.
