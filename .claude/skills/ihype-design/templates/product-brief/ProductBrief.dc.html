The gate belongs to the artists.

iHYPE is a live-music platform where fans hype the artists they love, buy tickets directly, and even earn by promoting shows — with 70% of every ticket locked to the artist, by charter, forever.

The problem

Ticketing giants take 30%+ in fees and own the fan relationship. Artists see little of the gate and none of the data. Discovery is pay-to-play.

The model

Every event publishes with a frozen charter — a split that can never change after sale. Fans buy direct. Promoters (any DJ or fan) earn from a dedicated 10% pool, never the artist's 70%. The only charge above face value is the card-processing fee (Stripe 2.9% + $0.30; AMEX 3.5% + $0.30), passed through at cost.
