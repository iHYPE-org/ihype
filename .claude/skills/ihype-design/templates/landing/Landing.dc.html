How it works

Discover, hype, buy direct.

Every step moves value from the middlemen back to artists and the fans who found them first.

01 · HYPE

Back the artists you believe in

A weekly flame budget lets you hype any artist, track, or show. The HYPE chart rises in real time.

02 · TICKETS

Buy direct, frozen charter

Every event publishes a split that can never change after sale. You see exactly where your money goes.

03 · PROMOTE

Spread the word, earn a cut

Share any event with your referral link. Earn up to 10% of the gate you drove — from the dedicated promoter slice, never the artist's.
