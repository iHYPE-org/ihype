iHYPE
Closed Beta · Tester Guide · v0.1.0-beta.5

Welcome to the iHYPE beta.

You're one of a small group testing artist-owned live music before anyone else. Here's how to get in, what to poke at, and how to tell us what breaks.

⚠ This is a work in progress

Things will be rough, slow, or broken. That's expected — and exactly what we need you to find. No real money moves yet; every purchase is simulated.

Getting in

1

Open the link we sent you on your phone (or desktop browser).

2

Enter your invite code — try IHYPE if yours doesn't work, or tap "Use demo code".

3

Pick a role and your taste in onboarding — or hit Skip → explore the demo to jump straight in.

What we'd love you to try

  • Hype an artist — tap the flame on Listen. You get a weekly budget; watch it count down.
  • Swipe Seeds — the discovery deck. Swipe right to hype, left to pass.
  • Buy a ticket — pick an event, run checkout end-to-end (it's simulated).
  • Share a referral link — as a DJ or Fan, promote an event and watch earnings accrue.
  • Build a radio show — DJs: crate some free-use tracks, record a voice intro, drop in SFX.
  • Catch a live show — join when an artist goes live; try the chat and hype pulse.

What's fake (don't report these)

  • All payments are simulated — no card is charged, no payout is real.
  • Artists, events, and tracks are seed data, not live bookings.
  • Listener counts and earnings are illustrative.
  • Push notifications may not actually arrive yet.

How to report

1

Tap the feedback button (bottom-right in the app) the moment something feels off.

2

Pick a type — Bug, Idea, or Love — and for bugs, a severity.

3

It auto-attaches the screen you were on + the build number. Just describe what you expected vs. what happened.

Questions? Reply to your invite email. Thank you for shaping iHYPE. 🔥
