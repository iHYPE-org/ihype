The gate belongs to the artists.

iHYPE is a live-music platform where fans hype the artists they love, buy tickets directly, and even earn by promoting shows — with 45% of every ticket locked to the artist, by charter, forever.

The problem

Ticketing giants take 30%+ in fees and own the fan relationship. Artists see little of the gate and none of the data. Discovery is pay-to-play.

The model

Every event publishes with a frozen charter — a split that can never change after sale. Fans buy direct. Promoters (any DJ or fan) earn a referral share drawn from the platform's slice, never the artist's.
