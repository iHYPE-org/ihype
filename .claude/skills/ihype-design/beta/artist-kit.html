The model

The gate split is frozen. Permanently.

When you publish an event, iHYPE locks your split into a charter record. No renegotiating, no clawbacks, no platform fees that quietly eat your share. The only charge above face value is the unavoidable card-processing fee (Stripe: 2.9% + $0.30; AMEX 3.5% + $0.30) — iHYPE takes $0.
