App name

iHYPE — Artist-Owned Live Music

Subtitle (30 char)

Hype · tickets · live shows

Promotional text

Closed beta now open. Hype the artists you love, buy tickets direct, and earn by promoting shows — with 70% of every ticket locked to the artist.

Description

iHYPE puts live music back in the hands of the people who make it.

Hype what you love — back rising artists and shape who blows up next.
Buy tickets direct — no scalper markup; 70% goes straight to the artist, locked by charter.
Earn as a promoter — any fan or DJ can share a referral link and earn up to 10% of the gate they drive.
Build radio shows — DJs mix from a free-use library with voice and royalty-free SFX.
Catch shows live — real-time listener counts, hype pulses, and live chat.

Beta: payments are simulated. iHYPE is a nonprofit building artist-owned music infrastructure.
