01 · How it works

Three inputs. One engine. Every output explainable.

A weighted blend you can audit at any time. Geographic relevance, social proof, and HYPE momentum — that's the whole formula. No black box. No paid boost.

Inputs

What we read

  • Your county & region
  • Content you've finished
  • HYPE on artists & shows
  • Momentum velocity (7-day)
→
The blend

Weights (public)

  • 40% taste match
  • 25% geographic weight
  • 20% social proof
  • 15% momentum
  • 0% paid placement
→
Outputs

What you see

  • Ranked artist feed (fans)
  • Show recommendations
  • Booking targets (venues)
  • Artist matches (promoters)
Auditable

Click "Why this?" on any recommendation and see the four signal scores that produced that ranking.

Anti-monoculture

Geographic weighting prevents global hits from crowding out your local scene. The closer the act, the heavier the weight.

No paid placement

Sponsored slots appear after organic results and are always labelled. No artist or label can buy a higher rank.

02 · Geographic cascade

Local first. Global last.

Every recommendation is weighted by proximity. The closer the source, the stronger the signal. This is what keeps your feed from becoming national algorithm soup.

×1.0
Local
Your county · ~30 mi
HYPE from your county counts at full weight. The strongest signal for small, local artists.
×0.7
Regional
Your state · ~150 mi
Drive-distance shows and regional acts. Where your scene tends to tour.
×0.4
National
Your country
Broad-market acts — discounted so they don't crowd out local shows.
×0.2
Global
Anywhere
International discoveries. Always available — never the dominant signal.
Adjusted HYPE = raw HYPE × geographic weight × recency factor
Geographic weight is determined by the distance tier shown above.
Recency factor decays linearly over 90 days — fresh HYPE counts more.

04 · Transparency by default

Every recommendation shows its math.

If you can't see why something was recommended, you can't know if you've been manipulated. iHYPE shows the four signals behind every ranking — and never hides paid placement.

4
Signal weights are public.
Taste 40%, geo 25%, social 20%, momentum 15%. Posted in the charter, with a changelog every time the board votes a change.
$0
Sponsored slots are labelled.
When a sponsored result reaches a recommendation surface it is tagged "Sponsored," appears after organic results, and never displaces them.
∞
You can audit any result.
Click "Why this?" on any recommendation. See the breakdown. Disagree? Flag it — flags route to public moderation review.
