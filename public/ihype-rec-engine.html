01 · How it works

One engine. Four inputs. Zero black box.

A weighted blend you can audit anytime. Geographic proximity, HYPE momentum, social proof, and taste — those are the only signals. Nothing pays for placement.

Inputs

What we read

  • Your county & region
  • Content you've finished
  • HYPE on artists & shows
  • Fan requests (venue lens)
  • Momentum velocity (7-day)

  • 25% geographic weight

  • 15% momentum velocity

Local-first

No paid boost

Sponsored content is labelled and appears after organic results. Money doesn't move rankings.

02 · Geographic cascade

Local first. Global last.

Every recommendation is weighted by proximity. The cascade applies to all four lenses — venues get an extra fan-request multiplier on top of it.

×1.0
Local
Your county · ~30 mi
Full weight. The strongest signal — smallest, closest artists surface first.
×0.7
Regional
Your state · ~150 mi
Drive-distance shows and regional acts. Where your scene tends to tour.
×0.4
National
Your country
Broad-market acts — discounted so they don't crowd out local shows.
×0.2
Global
Anywhere
International discoveries. Always reachable — never the dominant signal.
Adjusted score = base signal × geographic weight × recency factor
Recency decays linearly over 90 days. Fan requests add +5% to the venue lens only.

Every recommendation shows its math.
