The HYPE engine · how the platform breathes

One tap. Thirty days. The whole scene moves.

HYPE is how fans evangelize. Earn it by listening, attending, showing up. Spend it by hyping artists, venues, promoters, songs, albums, shows, and events worth knowing about — once every 30 days per source. HYPE fades over 90 days, so charts always reflect what's hitting right now.

01 · Anatomy

The HYPE button lives on every entity.

Three states, one rule: tap once per source per 30 days. Whether it's a song you finished, a venue you love, or a tour you can't wait for — same button, everywhere.

State 01 · Available

Tap to give HYPE

Outline icon shows the source is open. The number is the entity's current active HYPE — what's still inside its 90-day fade window.

State 02 · Hyped

You're on the board

Filled icon, count ticks up. Your HYPE is now part of this entity's score — fresh and weighted highest for the next 15 days.

State 03 · Cooldown

30-day source lock

You can still listen, watch, attend — but you can't double-stack HYPE on the same source. Refreshes 30 days after your last tap.

The 90-day fade horizon

Every HYPE you give starts hot and cools as it ages. Charts only contain HYPE that hasn't fully cooled.

Fresh 0–15d Hot 15–30d Warm 30–55d Cool 55–75d Fade 75–90d
Day 0 · 100% weight Day 45 · ~50% weight Day 90 · 0% · removed
